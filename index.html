Built With You · Run in Glass

A town you
can read.

A developer builds a town, sells you a flat, and keeps the books. Sūrya Nagari inverts all four: residents build it together, see every rupee, vote every decision, and own the whole open-source blueprint.

The Glass Treasury

Every rupee the town spends is on a public ledger any owner opens in the app — wages, harvests, repairs, compute sales. No closed books. A town you can read is a town you can trust.

Open Procurement

When the town needs anything built, SABHA posts the tender publicly; companies bid in the open, on the global Open Contracting standard. Every bid, every price, visible — procurement as a spectator sport.

Your Vote, in Your Pocket

Every decision that touches a value goes to residents through the app — Porto Alegre budgeting, Barcelona's Decidim, Taiwan's vTaiwan — woven into daily life, for a town small enough that every voice moves the outcome.
