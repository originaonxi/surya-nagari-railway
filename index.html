Your home

Live on a ring,
not in a block.

Six courtyard clusters of timber, solar-roofed homes around shared meadows — Sky Villas with plunge pools, Garden Homes, and Low-rise Residences. A garden at the centre of every ring, neighbours you chose, and no maintenance bill — because you own the infrastructure that maintains itself.

Health & everyday life

Cared for, never billed.

A continuous-care health model watches over every resident; doctors and an air-ambulance pad are on campus, for life. Around one ring: the university, the glass 24/7 kitchen, a theatre, the management tower and the zen garden — everything a community gathers around.

  • Illness caught before symptoms — predictive, preventive medicine
  • Food grown hours from your plate, ~85% on-site
  • Solar EVs, drones and robots handle the drudgery
The body is also a model

Olympic-grade,
every day.

A competition stadium and track, a 50-metre aquatics pool, a velodrome, courts, a cricket oval, an akhara and archery lanes — facilities most national athletes never get, free to every resident, woven into ordinary life. You move every day without ever "going to the gym."

Powered by the sun, run by AI

Electricity at ₹3.
Sold as intelligence.

22 MWp of sun-tracking panels, a 60 MWh battery hall, and two compute towers holding roughly an exaflop of green intelligence. The town makes the cheapest power in the industry and sells the surplus to the world as compute — so your costs never rise with inflation, and the place funds its own future.

32 acres of ahimsa

A tenth of the land wraps the whole town: a gaushala where cows retire and are never used, dog meadows, a bird wetland, a pollinator forest — and a circular lake of lotus between their world and ours. The town is guarded not by walls, but by life.

Built with you · run in glass · open to all

A town you can read.

The Glass Treasury

Every rupee the town spends is on a public ledger any owner opens in the app. No closed books — the Nordic principle that openness is what builds trust.

Open procurement

When the town needs anything built, the AI posts the tender and companies bid in the open. Every bid, every price, visible — procurement as a spectator sport.

Your vote, in your pocket

Every decision that touches a value goes to the residents through the app — participatory democracy proven from Barcelona to Taiwan, woven into daily life.

A living model built on the world's best — the Nordic welfare state, Swiss & Taiwanese digital democracy, participatory budgeting, open contracting — and fully open source.
