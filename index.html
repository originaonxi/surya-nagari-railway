How Ownership Works

One share.
You own the whole city.

Sūrya Nagari is structured as a Multi-State Cooperative Society (similar to how Amul works, but for a city). The cooperative owns every acre, every building, every factory, and every GPU. You buy a Class-A Membership Share and instantly become a co-owner of everything — not just a flat. The exact legal entity (Cooperative Society, Public Ltd., or hybrid trust structure) is under legal review — the ownership model remains identical regardless of final form.

🏛️

How the company works

Sūrya Nagari holds 100% of all land and assets through its cooperative structure. You hold a Class-A Membership Share. The cooperative pays for all services from its commercial revenues — so you never get a bill. This structure is similar to India's Amul dairy co-op, but applied to an entire planned city.

🔒

Non-speculative by design

Shares can be sold, but the company holds Right of First Refusal (ROFR). Resale prices are capped at inflation index + home improvements. No one can flip this to get rich at others' expense.

⚖️

What happens if you break the rules

Any proven act of physical violence results in share forfeiture. The company buys back the share at 70% of book value. The remaining 30% goes to the community welfare fund. The person vacates within 60 days.

🚪

How to exit

You can sell your shareblock at any time. The buyer must agree to the city's cultural charter. The company gets first right to buy it back at book value. Your family can also inherit the share.

Our Values · The Founding Constitution

A city with
a soul.

Sūrya Nagari is not just a financial structure. It is a living community built on Sanatan values — live and let live, harmlessness to all beings, and genuine care for every person and animal within these borders. These values are not suggestions. They are legally written into the company charter.

🌿

Pure Vegetarian City

No meat, no fish, no eggs — not in the homes, not in the restaurants, not imported. The city grows 100% plant-based food on its own farms. Leather and animal products are replaced by plant-based alternatives made in the Sūrya Works factories.

Written into the Articles of Association. Cannot be changed by any majority.
☮️

No Physical Violence

Any proven act of physical violence within the city triggers a mandatory review by the community tribunal. If confirmed, the shareholder's stake is forfeited — bought back at 70% of book value, with 30% to the community welfare fund. They vacate within 60 days.

Not a fine. Not a warning. The share is forfeited. This is how seriously we take it.
🐄

No Killing of Animals

No animal slaughter within or around the city — not for food, not for leather, not for research. The 600-acre ecological buffer protects native wildlife. Cows, dogs, birds and pollinators live in the outer ring as the city's guardians, not commodities.

Ahimsa — non-harming — is the first principle of Sanatan dharma. It is the law here.
🌞

Sanatan Culture — Live and Let Live

The city follows Sanatan principles: truth, non-violence, cleanliness, contentment, and self-discipline. No political parties. A merit-based Panchayat Council of 11 elected members governs. Candidates must show contribution to teaching, farming, or research to stand for election.

This is not a religion. It is a way of living that allows everyone to be free.
📖

Open Books — Glass Treasury

Every rupee the city spends is on a public ledger that every shareholder can read on their phone — wages, farm costs, hospital bills, GPU revenue, rental income. No closed books. Every major spending decision goes to a shareholder vote.

A city you can read is a city you can trust.
🔓

Open Source Blueprint

Everything — the city layout, the legal documents, the agricultural schedules, the energy systems, the governance protocols — is published under open-source licenses. Anyone can fork this blueprint and build their own version. This is not our city. It is a model for the world.

Fork it. Translate it. Build it in your country. That is the whole point.
