How You Earn SūryaCoins
Every contribution to the city is recorded on the public blockchain and rewarded. Farming, teaching, medical work, tech, maintenance, construction, governance participation, creative work. Vital roles like nursing and soil-building are the highest paid. You earn by giving. The more you give, the more you have.
The Quota System — Luxury for All
No one can buy out the whole theater. Every resident has a guaranteed monthly allotment of luxury access — regardless of how many coins they hold. Wealth does not exclude others. The richest and the quietest sit side by side at the concert.
The Glass Commonwealth

You run this city
together.

No elected politicians. No closed committees. No secret budgets. Every decision is made by live vote of all 10,000 shareholders. Every rupee is on a public blockchain every shareholder can read in real time.

🗳️
Live Voting — Every Decision
Expand the solar array? Build a new zone? Change a city policy? A proposal goes to all 10,000 residents simultaneously. You vote on the app. The result is live. No delay. No politics.
📖
The Glass Treasury
Every rupee the city spends is on a public ledger any shareholder opens in the app — wages, farm costs, GPU revenue, hospital bills, rental income. No closed books. A city you can read is a city you can trust.
🏛️
Panchayat Council — Earned, Not Elected
11 members, elected by all shareholders. Candidates must demonstrate real contribution — teaching, farming, medical work, or scientific research. You cannot buy your way to leadership here. You earn it by serving.
👷
Residents First — All Jobs
Every job in the city — from the hospital to the data center to the solar array — is offered to resident-shareholders first. You live here. You work here. The city's earnings flow back to you.
🔓
Open Source Everything
Every blueprint, every legal document, every engineering plan, every line of AI code — published on GitHub. Fork it. Build your own Sūrya Nagari. This is a model for civilization, not a product to sell.
⛓️
Blockchain — All Records
Every SūryaCoin transaction, every governance vote, every city contract, every share transfer — recorded on a public, immutable blockchain. No one can change the history. No one can hide the money.
