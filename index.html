Live Democracy · Your App · Every Decision

No politicians.
Just 10,000 humans and the truth.

When anything significant needs to change in the city, here is exactly what happens. It takes minutes. It is completely fair. It has never been done at this scale.

01
Someone sees a problem or possibility
Any resident submits a proposal on the app — build a new pavilion, change a policy, allocate resources differently, try a new farming method. No committee approval needed to propose.
02
AI prepares the honest picture
The AI gathers all relevant data — what it will cost in SūryaCoins, what it will impact, what similar decisions have resulted in elsewhere. It runs simulations: "If you vote yes, here is what the city looks like in 1 year, 5 years, 10 years."
No AI opinion. Only data. Only simulations. Only truth.
03
Every resident sees the neutral report
All 10,000 shareholders receive the proposal and AI's full data picture on their app. They can ask questions. The proposer can respond. The AI can be asked for more simulations. Everything is public.
04
The vote
Every resident votes. One share, one vote — always. The founder and the newest arrival vote with equal weight. The eldest elder and the youngest adult (18+) vote together. The result is live.
05
AI implements the decision
If the vote passes, the operational AI systems execute the decision. If it's a building project, the resources are allocated. If it's a policy change, it's encoded into the city's charter immediately. No delay. No bureaucracy.
Humans decide. AI builds. The cycle closes in days, not years.

What democracy was always supposed to be — before it was captured by parties and power.

The Shanti Sanctuary · Safety for Every Resident

Every home
is a fortress.

Complete safety is not paranoia. It is the foundation that makes genuine freedom possible. We follow Switzerland's model — 100% civilian shelter coverage — reimagined as living space, not fear space.

🏡
The Shanti Cellar
Personal bunker · Integrated into every home
Dual-use design — In peacetime: a soundproofed meditation room, creative studio, or library. The space lives as part of daily life. It is never a tomb.
Virtual sunrise ceiling — Micro-LED skylights simulate natural light cycles. The actual sky, underground. Zero claustrophobia.
Organic materials — Mycelium and local clay walls. The scent of rain-soaked earth. Warm, grounding, alive.
30-day life support — AI maintains food, water, air filtration, and power reserves. Tested quarterly while you sleep. Always ready.
City tunnel network — Every Shanti Cellar connects underground. During any surface emergency, you can move freely across the city below ground.
🌐
The Garbha
City central sanctuary · Underground · 50,000 people
Subterranean cathedrals — Basalt-arched caverns lit by bioluminescent moss and fiber-optic solar tubes. Real sunlight, underground.
Life continues below — Hot springs, theater spaces, community dining halls, and a full astronomical archive. If the world above needs time to settle, the world below flourishes.
Full city capacity — All 10,000 families plus visitors. Private quarters, common spaces, essential services replicated completely underground.
Independent systems — Separate power, separate water supply. Fully self-sufficient from the city above for extended periods.
Graceful activation — When AI detects any global threat, the transition is calm: ambient music, clear displays, transparent status. The city moves to safety the way it moves to morning yoga.
🇨🇭
Modelled on Switzerland — the country with 100% civilian shelter coverage for every citizen, maintained since 1963. The same country that has never been invaded. Complete safety is not a luxury.
