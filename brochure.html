You were not meant to spend

Commuting. Queuing. Cooking under pressure. Chasing bills, repairs and the next errand. An ordinary life spends thirty hours a week on the machinery of living — and almost none on actually living.

SABHAthe governance

Drafts budgets, publishes the receipts of everything the other seven did and why. Proposes, never decides — the council holds the only keys.

How the model

In the model's own words — the honest operating philosophy behind the town.

I run the boring parts invisibly, and publish everything. Irrigation, battery dispatch, drone routing, clinic scheduling, mart pricing — none of it deserves a human's morning. I take all of it. Sūrya Nagari · Detailed Prospectus14 · A Thousand Years
14 · Sized For A Thousand Years

Enough sun, water and soil —
for the generations after.

A township that promises "for life" must prove its arithmetic — not for 2,000 people this year, but for the seekers and generations who will fill these homes after them. Here is the honest budget, and the principle that makes it last.

Energy — the sun's daily budget

22 MWp of panels generate ≈ 35 GWh a year (~96 MWh a day) in Indian sun. The town draws ~24 MWh for homes, ~25 MWh for community, farms, water-treatment and EVs, and ~24 MWh for a full megawatt of GPU — about 73 MWh a day, leaving ~24% headroom, with the 60 MWh battery carrying the night. Comfortable for the founding 2,000 plus full compute — but not infinite. A thousand-year energy system is not bigger panels; it is a self-renewing field: every rooftop solar, land reserved now to triple toward ~60 MWp, hardware replaced perpetually from the town's own compute earnings, and the GPU as the elastic load — feasting when the sun is high, fasting at night.

Water — a closed loop, banked underground

200 acres catch ≈ 890,000 m³ of rain a year at India's average; ~470,000 m³ is harvestable. The town needs ~100,000 m³ domestic, ~200,000 m³ for drip-fed fields and recirculating farms, and ~30,000 m³ for the rest — about 330,000 m³ a year. Rain covers it with margin — but only as a closed loop: 90%+ greywater recycling, recirculating glasshouses, closed-loop liquid GPU cooling, the circular Water Ring as a reservoir, and aquifer recharge every monsoon so the underground bank is never drawn down.

2,000 founding ownership units — each a home, a seat, and a vote — sized primarily for individuals and couples. Every system is sized per person with headroom built in. The same footprint grows to 4,000–6,000 residents as households form across generations; 2,000 is the founding cohort, not the ceiling.
