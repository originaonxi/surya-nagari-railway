The Home-Building App · Satyug Collective

Choose your land.
Build your home.
Own everything.

An app like Uber — but for building your dream home on land where survival is already free. No algorithm. No middlemen. Owner-to-owner. Real-time GC quotes.

01
Browse availability — every vacant home allocation shown live, representing Class-A leasehold rights, sorted by listing date
02
Pick your allocation — size, zone, and co-op fees. See what Collective facilities are already there
03
Submit your home — 1BHK, 2BHK, 3BHK, custom. Your style, your budget
04
GC quotes arrive live — verified builders quote: price, timeline, materials. You choose
05
Contract + build — milestone-tracked, payment-scheduled, review after completion
The Rules — Non-Negotiable
✦
Land by owners only — no corporate listings, no aggregators, no flippers
✦
Sublet by members only — direct resident-to-resident, no external property managers, subject to CLT rules
✦
No algorithm — listings in chronological order. GCs alphabetical. No ranking engine. Ever.
✦
Member sets transfer price — the app never suggests or "optimises" your home transfer price, capped by anti-speculation rules
✦
100% verified reviews — only after a completed verified contract. Immutable. Not deletable.
✦
Paid = labeled — a paid GC gets one clearly-marked featured slot. Never a rank change
✦
You own the collective — every facility is member-owned, member-governed, member-benefiting
