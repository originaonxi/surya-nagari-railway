Conceptual Baseline · Proposed Access Models · Target Offsets Under Study

500 acres.
Target Net-Zero Cost Baseline.

The prototype is conceptually designed around shared baseline facilities funded prior to operations, aiming to study reduction of household cost anxiety. Projected operational costs are planned to be offset by commercial revenues (such as GPU compute leasing concepts), subject to technical feasibility, site selection, regulatory approvals, and member consensus.

☀️
Solar Farm + Battery
20 MW ground-mounted solar + 40 MWh battery storage. Conceptually sized to explore whether daytime generation could exceed baseline residential demand. Surplus energy is modeled to offset the grid-tied GPU campus where policy allows; any member distribution depends on audited surplus.
Proposed Baseline
🍽️
Community Kitchen
Proposed baseline targeting three organic vegetarian meals daily, designed to be prepared in zone kitchens from farm yields. Fresh, seasonal. Intended to alleviate daily cooking obligations.
Proposed Baseline

Clean Water System
Rainwater harvesting, deep aquifer recharge, greywater recycling, and closed-loop sewage treatment. Designed to explore closed-loop water delivery targeting every home.
Proposed Baseline

Community Farms
Planned allocation of up to 200 acres of organic open-field and vertical farming. Grains, dal, vegetables, fruits, herbs, spices. Sourced locally or cultivated inside the Collective, targeting shorter local food supply chains.

The Collective Lake
The Central Lake · 15 Acres · Heart of the Prototype

Not a feature.
A soul.

The 500-acre prototype layout proposes a 15-acre man-made lake at its centre. Designed with the goal that every zone touches it and every resident is within walking distance.

The Home-Building App · Satyug Collective

Choose your land.
Build your home.
Sovereign leasehold design.

A conceptual app design for coordinating home construction in a peer-to-peer building environment. Subject to development and member approval.

01
Browse proposed availability — proposed vacant allocations may be shown, representing Class-A leasehold rights, sorted by listing date
02
Select allocation — size, zone, and co-op fees. Review planned Collective facilities nearby
03
Submit home concept — 1BHK, 2BHK, 3BHK, custom. Your style, your budget
04
Bidding concept — verified builders quote: price, timeline, materials. You choose
05
Proposed contract & build — milestone-tracked, payment-scheduled, review after completion
Proposed Governance Rules
✦
Land by owners only — no corporate listings, no aggregators, no flippers
✦
Sublet by members only — direct resident-to-resident, no external property managers, subject to CLT rules
✦
Design Concept — listings sorted chronologically. GCs listed alphabetically. Intended to avoid ranking-based placement.
✦
Member sets transfer price — the proposed app design does not suggest or "optimise" your home transfer price, capped by anti-speculation rules
✦
Planned reviews — subject to moderations and verification guidelines.
✦
Paid = labeled — a paid GC is proposed to receive a clearly-marked featured slot, with no ranking changes.
✦
Proposed member ownership — every facility is proposed to be member-owned, member-governed, member-benefiting

Build Timeline · 500-Acre Prototype

Proposed Four-Year Timeline.

Each phase is proposed to function as an active Collective — not a construction site waiting to be finished.

Year 1
Proposed land acquisition. Proposed MSCS formation. Target first 200 founding families. Proposed lake excavation. Solar + battery target. Proposed community kitchen and clean water setup.
Year 2
Target 500 homes complete. Proposed wellness center (lifestyle disciplines). Proposed K–12 school. Proposed EV transport options. Target farms at 60% capacity. Proposed GPU campus (first 128 GPUs). App concept v1.0.
Year 3
Target 1,000 homes. Proposed lake completion. Proposed 256 GPUs. Target farm output. Proposed eco-textile and tool library. Potential audited surplus distributions, if any, are subject to reserve funding and member general body decision.
Year 4
Prototype model evaluation. Documentation and open-source release targeted where feasible. App concept v2.0 — Satyug Collective platform reference. Fork #1 of the blueprint targeted to begin somewhere in India.
