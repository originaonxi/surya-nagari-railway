The 3 Nights · 4 Days Enrichment Package

Not a pitch deck.
A lived demonstration.

Anyone can describe a future city. The Satyug miniature must be felt. Qualified founding members and families are invited to spend four days inside the prototype: eating from the farm, working from the AI café, sitting in silence, learning with children, cooking with the kitchen team, and seeing how 15–20 people can live beautifully together.

Proposed model: Free for serious pre-qualified founding members, families, doctors, educators, architects, organic farming operators, and long-horizon builders. This is not tourism. It is cooperative due diligence through lived experience. Cooperative memberships represent governance and shared baseline usage, not a registered security or guaranteed yield-producing asset.

Uttarakhand Miniature · Location Shortlist

The first Satyug should be
offbeat, fertile, sunny, and protected.

The 500-acre prototype and the smaller 15–20 person miniature both need low-density agricultural land, strong sun for solar, natural water channels, road access, privacy, and long-term growth without mass-tourism pressure. These regions are a shortlist for field verification — not final recommendations.

01 · Nainital Foothills
Kotabagh & Taliya Village
A lush foothill valley outside crowded tourist cores. Strong fit for a first miniature because the terrain is friendlier for farms, rainwater harvesting, and low-impact structures. Lower elevation also helps reduce winter heating and server-cooling extremes.
Founder VisitsFarm StayRoad Access
02 · Near Mukteshwar
Ramgarh & Nathuakhan Valley
The Fruit Bowl of Kumaon. Organic farming culture, sun-drenched valley character, wellness seekers, digital nomads, and strong visual identity. Good fit for the AI + slow-living + orchard version of Satyug.
Organic FarmsSolarSlow Living
03 · Almora District
Jageshwar & Artola Belt
A spiritual and deodar-forest region with strong wellness resonance. Best fit if the prototype leans heavily into Ayurveda, silence, temple culture, spa, retreat, and holistic mothering.
SpiritualWellnessRetreat
04 · Champawat District
Lohaghat
Long-horizon land-banking candidate: quieter, more off-radar, potentially larger parcels, and deeper blank-canvas potential. Better for the 10-year 500-acre/5,000-acre arc than an immediate luxury visitor prototype.

MBHK · Mother BHK · Residential Care Concept

Mothering should feel
like luxury and nature together.

The MBHK is proposed as a 51-day residential mothering environment within Satyug: final-month arrival, postnatal recovery, Ayurvedic malish, farm nutrition, new mother circles, baby photos, and a husband work zone. It is not a hospital. It follows your doctor’s plan.

30 + 21 = 51 days

Come in the ninth month. Stay until at least 21 days after delivery. Your own licensed doctor leads clinical decisions. The Satyug team provides the environment: MBHK apartment, farm food, Ayurvedic care approved by your doctor, and a circle of other new mothers.

Proposed capacity begins with 10 mothers per month. Small enough that every mother is known by name. Large enough that no mother is alone.

Medical framing: Any implementation requires licensed obstetric/neonatal oversight, registered facility partnerships, emergency transfer protocols, and compliance with all Indian medical regulations.
MBHK: a fully-loaded 1BHK residential suite you can decorate and live in with your baby.
Plant Café: farm-grown meals and cold-pressed juices following your doctor-advised diet.
Daily Malish: mother and baby Ayurvedic massage by trained practitioners, approved by your doctor.
Mother Circle: daily conversation with other new mothers — because new mothers should talk to each other.
Husband Zone: 5G/1 Gbps work café, standing desks, gym, protein bar, swimming, and quiet presence.

English is the language of our time; Sanskrit carries the depth of Advaita, yoga, Ayurveda, and Indian thought; Greek carries philosophy, logic, drama, politics, and the classical foundations of Western reason. AI lets every child access all of it.
