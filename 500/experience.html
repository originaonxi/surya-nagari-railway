Explain it to a farmer

It is a village where people stop struggling alone.

In a normal life, every family pays separately for electricity, food, water, school, health, internet, repairs, and transport.

In Satyug, families try to share the cost of building and maintaining many of these things together as a community.

The farm gives food. The sun gives electricity. Rainwater is saved. Children learn. Mothers are cared for. People work online. Evenings become human again.

Family rest care

A gentle family rest cottage idea: a peaceful place where a mother can recover, eat well, rest, and be supported after clinical care happens through proper doctors and facilities.

Care with dignity
Why Uttarakhand?

Mountains, farms, water, sun, and silence.

We are not saying these places are final. They are places to go, walk, ask, test water, test roads, understand land laws, and see where the first small Satyug farm could be born.

Kotabagh / Taliya

A foothill valley. Easier farm feeling. Good for the first small sample where guests can come and understand the life.

Ramgarh / Nathuakhan

Fruit, orchards, slow living, and wellness feeling. Good for families and people who want farm life with beauty.

Jageshwar / Artola

More spiritual, forested, quiet. Good if the first farm leans toward Ayurveda, silence, mother care, and retreat.

Lohaghat

More untouched and long-term. Maybe better for bigger land thinking later, not necessarily the first visitor farm.

Family Rest Cottage, in simple words

A new mother should not recover alone.

A peaceful cottage around the family.

A family may stay in a peaceful 1BHK-style cottage around the birth period. After the baby is born with licensed medical professionals at an appropriate facility, the mother rests in the cottage for at least 21 days.

Satyug does not replace doctors, nurses, midwives, or hospitals. This cottage provides only accommodation and environment: food, rest, doctor-approved malish, other mothers to talk to, and a husband zone so the family can stay together.

Family cottage: a warm private home-like space, not a hospital ward and not a medical facility.
Food: farm meals and juices following her doctor's diet.
Malish: Ayurvedic massage only if medically allowed by the family’s doctor.
Other mothers: daily circles so no one feels alone.
Husband zone: internet, work desk, gym, café, and presence.

Small clear notes

This page explains a proposed concept in simple language. It is not a securities offer, not a promise of income, and not an invitation to buy land without legal due diligence.

Any Uttarakhand land work must follow state land laws, land ceiling rules, land-use permissions, environmental clearances, and proper legal checks.

The family rest cottage is not a hospital. No medical, midwifery, nursing, or delivery services are provided on-site unless separately licensed under law. Birth and clinical decisions must be handled by licensed doctors and registered clinical facilities. Satyug only describes accommodation, food, rest, and the surrounding care environment.
