Proposed Platform Principles

The Proposed No-Algorithm Concept

Designed to protect the community marketplace from ranking manipulation, artificial inflation, and opaque developer markups.

Charter Constraint Proposed Code Enforcement Intended Benefit to Community
Chronological Sort Listings are designed to sort strictly by `listed_at` timestamp with no ranking boost. Mitigates commercial flipping and pay-to-play visibility.
Alphabetical Contractors Builders are proposed to be listed alphabetically or via custom filters. No system scores. Aims to prevent review gaming and platform recommendation bias.
100% Owner-Set Prices Platform lacks automatic valuation engines or pricing recommendations. Designed to prevent algorithmically driven price escalation to protect buyers.
Escrow-Locked Bidding Proposed contract structure signs, hashes, and releases funds on milestone approvals. Designed to mitigate risks of builder delays and payment disputes.
Verified Reviews Only Feedback forms are proposed to unlock strictly upon settled, completed contracts. Designed to eliminate unverified review manipulation.
